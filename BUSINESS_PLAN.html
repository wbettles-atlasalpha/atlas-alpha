Business Plan: Atlas Alpha

The Systematic Intelligence House

1. Executive Summary

Atlas Alpha is a research-first intelligence house that provides high-net-worth (HNW) traders with systematic market insights. By utilizing our proprietary "Earnings Drift" engine, we identify post-catalyst institutional accumulation. We offer a dual-channel intelligence product (D2C Signal Feed + Boutique Intelligence) designed to scale while maintaining high-touch value.

2. The Market Opportunity

  • The Noise Problem: Retail investors are overwhelmed by speculative news and social media noise.
  • The Signal Gap: Institutional-grade quantitative analysis is gated behind expensive Bloomberg terminals or inaccessible hedge fund structures.
  • Target Market: The HNW professional (30-50, tech/finance/entrepreneurial). They value time-efficiency, disciplined risk management, and quantitative proof over anecdotal "tips."

3. The Product & The Moat

  • Earnings Drift Engine: Our proprietary algorithm that flags institutional accumulation events.
  • The Invalidation Thesis (The Safety Engine): We focus on when to get out. By mathematically defining the "Invalidation Thesis," we remove the emotional burden from the trader.
  • Technical Moat: Our engine is a proprietary, local-first intelligence architecture that we control—not a copyable cloud SaaS.

4. Revenue Model

  • D2C Signal Feed (The Engine - $199/mo): High-frequency, actionable data signals for serious traders.
  • Boutique Intelligence (The Lab - $999/mo): Deep-dive research, systematic quant reports, and access to our Community Research Lab (Discord).
  • Growth Path: Target 500 Pro subscribers (D2C) to validate Alpha, then leverage that data to scale into Family Office/RIA partnerships.

5. Regulatory Strategy

Atlas Alpha is a Research & Intelligence firm, not an investment advisor.

  • We deliver data, not trade recommendations.
  • Every output is framed as a "Quantitative Metric" for user research.
  • We utilize automated disclaimers to ensure compliance.

6. Go-To-Market (GTM)

  • D2C Funnel: Content-led acquisition (Twitter/LinkedIn/Niche Trading Forums).
  • Community Lab: Our Discord "Research Lab" acts as the primary retention vehicle, building a community of disciplined traders who benchmark against each other.
  • Referral Flywheel: High-value intelligence incentivizes existing users to bring in high-quality peers.

7. Operational Maturity

  • Execution Architecture: We have moved to a lean, platform-agnostic IBKR engine.
  • Rigorous QA: Post-incident audits ensure our "Intelligence House" is built on robust, bug-free foundations.

8. The Ask

We are seeking seed-stage funding to scale data infrastructure, formalize our research delivery platform, and solidify regulatory compliance as a boutique intelligence firm.
