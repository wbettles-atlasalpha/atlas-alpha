# Atlas Alpha - Experimental Hypotheses

We are running a process of elimination to isolate institutional accumulation patterns ("The Secret Sauce"). 

## Active Test: The Institutional Footprint
- **Condition:** 5x-10x Volume spike, >5% Sector Relative Strength, No-News catalyst.
- **Logic:** Identifies large block accumulation before market participants process news.

## Future Hypotheses (If Footprint test fails)

### 1. The Earnings Drift (Momentum Persistence)
- **Logic:** Institutional desks cannot build a massive position in a single day. We look for stocks that gap up 3-5% on earnings and sustain that gap for 10+ days without filling it.
- **Hypothesis:** Sustained "Gap Integrity" indicates institutional accumulation that takes weeks to complete.

### 2. High Short Interest Reversal (The Squeeze Proxy)
- **Logic:** Track mid-caps with >20% short interest. Scan for an Alpha Vector trigger (Volume spike) during a period of market-wide strength.
- **Hypothesis:** When short-sellers are forced to cover in a high-growth environment, the liquidity shift creates explosive, non-linear returns.

### 3. Volatility Compression (The Bollinger Squeeze)
- **Logic:** Scan for assets where the 20-day volatility has compressed to a 6-month low, followed by a volume-backed break above the upper Bollinger Band.
- **Hypothesis:** A long period of inactivity followed by a violent volume expansion is the classic "coiled spring" pattern for explosive mid-cap growth.

### 4. Insider/Buyback Proxy
- **Logic:** Monitor for "buyback-like" price behavior in tickers during periods of depressed valuation (<$10B market cap).
- **Hypothesis:** Institutional buyers often accumulate heavily when they believe their stock is structurally undervalued, even if "the market" hasn't realized it yet.
