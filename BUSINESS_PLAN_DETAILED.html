Atlas Alpha: Investment Prospectus

1. Executive Summary

Atlas Alpha is a systematic investment intelligence firm. We solve the primary pain point of modern HNW traders: the gap between information noise and actionable signal. Our proprietary Earnings Drift Engine quantifies institutional accumulation post-catalyst, providing users with a repeatable edge and the rigorous risk-management framework (The Invalidation Thesis) necessary to retain it.

2. Market Analysis (TAM/SAM/SOM)

The global retail trading market is undergoing a "quantization" phase. High-net-worth professionals are moving away from manual speculation toward systematic trading, yet institutional tools remain out of reach. We operate at the intersection of retail accessibility and institutional methodology.

3. Product Architecture

The Earnings Drift Engine

Our core IP is a local-first, proprietary quant algorithm that processes market data to identify institutional footprint. Unlike standard technical indicators, our Drift criteria are calibrated against historical post-earnings volatility profiles.

The Invalidation Thesis (The Safety Engine)

We monetize discipline. The "Invalidation Thesis" is our core product feature; we define mathematical exit criteria for every trade, removing the psychological friction that causes 90% of retail traders to fail.

5. Regulatory Compliance & Risk

Atlas Alpha provides quantitative research, not investment advice. Our entire product architecture is built to ensure a clear distinction:

  • Automated Disclaimers: User acknowledgment required at every point of entry.
  • Quantitative Framing: Signals are delivered as "data metrics," not "recommendations."
  • Non-Custodial: We do not hold user funds or manage assets directly.
